Listing:

  $ gpg --list-keys EMAIL

Exporting:

  $ gpg --armor --export EMAIL > pub.gpg

  $ gpg --armor --export-secret-keys EMAIL > priv.gpg

  $ gpg --gen-revoke EMAIL > revoke.crt

  $ gpg --fingerprint EMAIL

  $ gpg --send-keys ID

Importing:

  $ gpg --import pub.gpg

  $ gpg --allow-secret-key-import --import priv.gpg

  $ gpg --receive-keys ID

  $ gpg --refresh-keys

Signing:

  $ gpg --sign-key EMAIL

Encrypting:

  $ gpg -c FILE

  $ gpg [--armor] -es -r RECEIVER [-r RECEIVER ...] FILE

Decrypting:

  $ gpg FILE
